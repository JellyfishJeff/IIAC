A place for your medical history and your health improvement.


IIAC, Information In A Click is a system built for developing countries to augment medical service efficiency. Health care is still growing in developing countries and has a long way to go. Nevertheless, with tools like IIAC built for individuals as well as instituions, one can now educate himself on existing conditions and keep track of his or her medical records. On a bigger scale, IIAC is used by different medical institutions established in developing countries to serve as some sort of HIS, Hospital Information System and RIS, Radiology Information System and effectively keep a record of thousands of different patients accross the world.

Individual Usage



Educate Yourself on your Medical Conditions and Beyond

Access your personal medical records in just a click no matter your location! IIAC allows any individual store files ranging from prescriptions, operations, surgeries, dates, medications to images or even suggesting coping groups nearby or ways to enhance diets or exercise to accomodate any and every condition a patient may face.

Accessing medical records is key but the IIAC's interest lays in the actual improvement of an individual's condition or disease. Regardless of location, patients are able to continually improve on their convalescence using the features listed above offered by IIAC.

Uploading your records is just as easy as uploading a picture on facebook. Using an integrated scanning tool, the IIAC is able to convert prescriptions, files with signatures, certificates, x-ray images and more into understandable and detailed files stored in your account. Our terms and conditions list how your information is used and stored for you. Only with the users permission can the information be used for statistics and data collection.

The website being user friendly really aids the users navigate the website as easily as possible. One's IIAC content is specific to them. Using information inputed by the user on a profile section, IIAC is able to help a patient enhance his or her medical experience drastically.



Institutional Usage



A Huge Network of Medical Institutions is in Progress

Different institutions registered undered IIAC can store their loads records for thousands of patients. Gaining premium information about trneding diseases or epidemics, third world countries medical institutions can enhance their programs and services. At IIAC we believe knowledge is power and once trained doctors have access to the knowledge and facilities and tools like this website which they need, they'll be unstoppable. Improving health care services is the primary focus of IIAC as the health care sector is often neglected in developing countries.

Medical Institutions having access to every single patient they come across will help build up data for future references. Networking across the globe, institutions can access different databases and effectively help their patients as they have utterly accurate information about the patient they are treating at the second. This will be a future feature of the IIAC as the primary focus is the individual's accessibility to his/her own medical records.
