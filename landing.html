Access Medical Records and Information in a Single Click



IIAC, Information In A Click is a system built for developing countries to augment medical service efficiency. Health care is still growing in third world countries and has a long way to go. Nevertheless, with tools like IIAC built for individuals as well as instituions, one can now educate himself on existing conditions and keep track of his or her medical records. On a bigger scale, IIAC is used by different medical institutions established in third world countries to serve as some sort of HIS, Hospital Information System and RIS, Radiology Information System and effectively keep a record of thousands of different patients accross the world.



Premium Access to Medical Information



Premium Access to Medical Information and General Knowledge

Statistics and numbers convey factual Information. With access to up to date medical information on different conditions increase the flexibilty and broadness covered by the IIAC website. Knowing how many people are affected by the same condition often helps a patient. General Information about one's condition is avaible as well. Once a user is logged in, his feed is made up of facts, Q&A's about his condition sourced from top medical resources and any other personal interest in learning about other diseases ranging from chicken pox to cancer. Despite having access to premium international medical information IIAC's primary focus is third world countries thus contributing to the efforts put in place by its approach to educate and better these not so fortunate countries.
