About Us


Moving from Cameroon to America, I have always had it in heart to better my motherland through my intellectual abilities. The IIAC Co. is a group of two Cameroonian coming together to improve and enhance health care in not only Cameroon but in every third world country. Primarily located in Indianapolis, Indiana, with a team of three, IIAC serves its purpose based on four core values; Civility, Order, Respect and Excellence. Putting our client first and providing access to premium information using elaborated designs always displays our sense of excellence.
