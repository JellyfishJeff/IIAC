My Records

The IIAC provides a cloud storage place for any sort of medically approved files. This allows a patient to upload an x-ray image done or just a prespriction from a doctor. Patients will have access to these anytime they're logged into IIAC's website.

My Medications

Knowing the right medication is vital for any sort of patient despite the seriousness of their disease or condition. "My medication" helps patients keep track of their medicines and provides details about each one; what it does, when it was prescribed, what hospital prescribed it and more. This is helpful for patients who visit different institutions so their medical history is clear and concise.

My Reminders

An entry system to create a log of different medications and medical appointments established by the patient himself is available here. Reminders are crucial for most people and having a website that doesn't only compile all of your medical information in one place but also helps you keep track of your conditions to get better is a need in the 21st century. Poeple may have so much going on that they tend to forget what matters which is why IIAC got your back.
